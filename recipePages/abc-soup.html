ABC-Soup

Ingredient List

  • 1 pound bone-in chicken, cut to pieces
  • 4 cups water, more as needed
  • 1 large carrot, peeled and cut into chunks
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon crushed black peppercorns, or pepper
  • 2 - 3 large potatoes
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Prepare the ingredients.
  2. Put the chicken pieces in a medium saucepan with just enough water to cover. Cover and bring to a boil.
  3. Drain the water. Rinse the chicken under water to get rid of any impurities.
  4. Fill a medium soup pot with 4 cups of water. Add the prepared chicken, cover, and bring to a boil over medium heat.
  5. Once the water boils, remove any impurities from the surface with a ladle. Adjust the heat to low.
  6. Add the carrots, onion and peppercorn. Simmer on very low heat for 1 and a half hours.
  7. Add the potatoes to the soup. Continue to simmer till the chicken is cooked through and the carrots and potatoes are tender. This should take about 2 and a half hours.
  8. Add salt to taste, and serve.

Conclusion

Comfort food.
